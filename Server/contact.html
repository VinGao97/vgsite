Towards a library without walls.

Are.na talks about a world where information is accessible to all. A library of information and data without boundaries sounds like what the internet should be. Are.na already took a step towards that with their service operating within a community of artists and culturally-engaged individuals that provide such information and works to their users. The comparison between the Memex and the database of are.na is a bit confusing to me. The concept is the same but what exactly is a Memex? Compared to Are.na, Memex only allows one on one exchanges where Are.na gathers information and works through crowd sourcing which to me seems like a better way in gathering and distributing information to a large audience.
